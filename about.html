About Us

PHS Falcons Esports

The PHS Esports Team & Club gives students a place to compete, collaborate, and connect through gaming. The program supports both serious varsity competition and casual club participation.

Our Team

PHS Esports is built around teamwork, communication, respect, and school spirit. Students can represent the school through varsity Rocket League or join the casual club to play and discuss games with classmates.

Important Members

Mr. Broome

Mr. Broome is the teacher sponsor for the PHS Esports Team & Club. He helps support the club at PHS, provides adult supervision, assists with school communication, and helps students keep the program organized and school-appropriate.

Availability: Students should check with Mr. Broome during posted school availability times or contact him through the school-approved communication method.

Brendan Choulas

Brendan Choulas is the student captain of the PHS Esports Team. He helps coordinate varsity Rocket League information, supports club communication, and helps guide students who are interested in competitive or casual participation.

Availability: Students can reach out during club meetings or through the official interest/contact form when available.

The Garfielders

The Garfielders are the four-person student development team that coded the PHS Esports Team & Club website. The team includes Jainavaneeth Kumarashankar, Megan Lo, Nathan Parra, and Sarayu Penchala.

As the website development team, the Garfielders helped turn the club requirements into a usable multi-page website with schedules, announcements, highlights, sign-up access, and clear navigation for students interested in esports.
